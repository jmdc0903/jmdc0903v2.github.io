About Us

Welcome to PHcuisine

Embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of the Philippines with PHcuisine. Our passion for authentic Filipino cuisine is matched only by our dedication to delivering exceptional dining experiences. From traditional classics to innovative creations, each dish tells a story of rich heritage and cultural diversity.

At PHcuisine, we believe that great food brings people together. With a commitment to quality ingredients, meticulous preparation, and unparalleled hospitality, we strive to exceed your expectations with every bite. Whether you're craving the comforting warmth of adobo or the tantalizing sweetness of halo-halo, our menu is sure to delight your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

Special Fan Cake | Hot Choco Php200
Indulge in the heavenly combination of our Special Fan Cake accompanied by a piping hot cup of cocoa. The fluffy texture of the cake, paired with the rich, velvety smoothness of the hot chocolate, creates a delightful symphony of flavors that will tantalize your taste buds.
Special Beef Pares Php250
Savor the exquisite flavors of our Beef Pares, a beloved Filipino dish renowned for its tender meat and savory-sweet sauce. Slow-cooked to perfection, each bite of succulent beef paired with fragrant fried rice offers a taste of comfort and satisfaction.
Ensaymada | Hot Choco Php220
Treat yourself to the classic Filipino pastry, Ensaymada, paired with a comforting cup of hot chocolate. With its soft, buttery texture and generous sprinkling of cheese and sugar on top, each bite of Ensaymada is a delightful indulgence.
Special Beef Mami Php250
Experience the perfect harmony of flavors in our Mami Siopao, a traditional Filipino comfort food. This steamed bun filled with savory meat and served with a side of flavorful broth is sure to warm your heart and satisfy your cravings.
Taho | Suman Php200
Dive into the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines with our Taho and Suman combo. Enjoy the silky smoothness of Taho, a sweet soybean pudding topped with caramelized syrup, paired with the delightful chewiness of Suman, a traditional sticky rice cake wrapped in banana leaves.
Lugaw / Arroz Caldo / Goto Php220
Indulge in the comforting warmth of our Lugaw, Arroz Caldo, or Goto, three classic Filipino rice porridge dishes. Each spoonful of these hearty soups, made with tender meat, rice, and aromatic spices, offers a taste of home and nourishment for the soul.
Bibingka | Tsaa Php200
Transport yourself to the streets of the Philippines with our Bibingka and Tsaa combo. Made from glutinous rice flour and coconut milk, Bibingka is a beloved Filipino rice cake traditionally enjoyed during festive occasions. Paired with a cup of fragrant tea, this sweet and savory treat is a delightful indulgence.
Silog Meals Php250
Experience the ultimate Filipino breakfast with our Silog Meals, a combination of garlic fried rice, fried egg, and your choice of meat such as tocino, tapa, longganisa, or bangus. Bursting with flavor and served with a side of vinegar dipping sauce, our Silog Meals are a hearty and satisfying way to start your day.
Special Bulalo Php400
Indulge in the culinary delight of our signature Special Bulalo—a Filipino comfort food classic with a twist. Our hearty beef shank soup is simmered to perfection, infused with aromatic herbs and spices, and served piping hot with tender meat and flavorful marrow.
Special Chicken Adobo Php350
Experience the savory sensation of our Special Chicken Adobo—a timeless Filipino favorite with a unique twist. Our tender chicken pieces are marinated in a harmonious blend of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, then slow-cooked to perfection until they reach melt-in-your-mouth perfection. Each bite is a symphony of flavors, combining tangy acidity with savory depth, creating a culinary masterpiece that's sure to tantalize your taste buds.
Grilled Pusit Php380
Savor the irresistible flavor of our Grilled Pusit—a seafood delicacy that's sure to tantalize your taste buds. Fresh squid, expertly marinated in a blend of herbs, spices, and citrus, is charred to perfection over an open flame, resulting in a succulent and tender dish with a delightful smoky aroma. Each bite is a symphony of textures, from the tender flesh to the slightly crispy edges, offering a satisfying experience that captures the essence of coastal cuisine.
